Who We Are

Empowering the Future Women Scientists

Hamda Science Academy, managed by the esteemed Markazuda'wa Thil Islamiyya Thazhapra, provides a comprehensive, highly focused residential environment specifically designed for female students to excel in Plus Two Science studies and highly competitive medical and engineering entrances.

We believe that modern science combined with moral integrity creates an unbeatable foundation. Our secure campus allows girls to study with full freedom, focus, and state-of-the-art facilities.

  • Expert Faculty from premier national institutions
  • Individualized mentorship and round-the-clock guidance
  • Complete secure female residential and learning setup
