Our Heritage & Vision

Empowering outstanding female science students through a synthesis of top-tier academic training and strong moral ethics.

Markazuda'wa Thil Islamiyya Thazhapra

Hamda Science Academy was established and is managed directly by the renowned charitable educational trust, Markazuda'wa Thil Islamiyya Thazhapra. Our organization is dedicated to bridging the educational disparity and introducing premium scientific academies with strong moral standards.

Recognizing the immense potential of female students in regional districts, the management envisioned an oasis where girls could prepare for highly taxing engineering and medical tests under a secure, fully protective and moral residential setting.

At Hamda, we eliminate external distractions. Our teaching methodology involves meticulous scheduling, expert daily analysis, conceptual clearing lectures, and regular mockup test exercises, preparing students to take their exams with supreme confidence.

Our Vision

To be the national benchmark in female-only senior secondary science and competitive career empowerment.

Our Mission

Delivering expert integrated curricula, nurturing moral integrity, and offering a peaceful learning sanctuary.

Hostel & Living

A Secure Residential Oasis

We understand that academic productivity is profoundly impacted by a student's living environment. Our campus provides a premium, fully residential **Girls-Only Hostel** designed to be a comfortable "home away from home."

24/7 Monitored Surveillance: Complete boundary walling, secure access controls, and active supervision guarantee total security.
Nutritious Dining: Serving fresh, clean, and nutritious vegetarian and non-vegetarian food customized to optimize physical and cognitive health.
Dedicated Female Staff: Wardens, medical assistants, kitchen workers, and security supervisors are fully represented by female personnel, ensuring absolute comfort.
Spiritual and Moral Grounding: Regular ethical sessions, spiritual classes, and moral guidelines cultivate character and empathy.
